Support the Dignity Project

There are girls and women struggling to attend school, go to work, complete errands, and participate in social activities due to menstrual hygiene product insecurity and our goal is to help alleviate this roadblock for these individuals. We are seeking groups and organizations who would like to partner with us by making product or monetary donations. The most-needed product donations are regular and overnight sanitary napkins, regular and super tampons, panty liners, and ½ gallon storage bags.
