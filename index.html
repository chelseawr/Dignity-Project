Our Mission

❤

Dignity for All

We believe that access to menstrual hygiene products is a basic human right, not a luxury. No woman or girl should have to choose between her health and her daily life because of something as natural as menstruation.

★

Breaking Barriers

Period poverty affects millions worldwide, keeping girls out of school and women out of the workforce. We work to eliminate those barriers through direct product distribution, education, and community advocacy.

♨

Building Community

Through partnerships with local shelters, schools, and community organizations, we create lasting networks of support that ensure no one faces period poverty alone.

Who We Support

Our donations reach women and girls who need them most, through trusted community partners.

01

Local Shelters

We partner with domestic violence shelters and homeless shelters across the region to provide monthly supplies of menstrual hygiene products to women in crisis situations.

02

Schools & Universities

Our school program ensures that no student misses class because of her period. We stock free product dispensers in restrooms and provide take-home kits for students in need.

03

Community Centers

We work with community organizations in underserved neighborhoods to distribute products and provide education around menstrual health and hygiene.

04

International Partners

Through our global network, we support menstrual health initiatives in developing communities where access to products is most limited and stigma is greatest.
